Privacy Policy – Where Is My Driver

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Where Is My Driver ("the app", "we") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

The app collects the following data only while the driver actively uses it:

  • Precise location (GPS) – collected continuously while sharing is active, to display the driver's real-time position to passengers.
  • Driver name – entered voluntarily by the driver in Settings; displayed to passengers on the tracking page.
  • Driver phone number – entered voluntarily by the driver in Settings; displayed to passengers on the tracking page.

No account registration is required. Passengers do not need to install the app or provide any personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

All collected data is used solely to enable the app's core feature: real-time location sharing between the driver and passengers. Specifically:

  • GPS location is transmitted to our backend (Supabase) and made available to anyone who has the unique tracking link generated for the session.
  • Driver name and phone number are stored in our backend and displayed on the passenger's tracking page.

We do not use this data for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

3. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored on Supabase (supabase.com), a secure cloud database. All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit). Session data is associated with a unique session ID and is overwritten when a new session starts.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third-party companies or advertisers. Location, name, and phone number are accessible only to passengers who possess the unique tracking link shared by the driver.

5. Data Retention

Location data is updated in real time and overwritten with each new session. No historical location logs are retained. Driver name and phone number remain in the database until the driver clears or updates them in Settings.

6. Your Choices

  • The driver can stop sharing their location at any time by tapping "Stop Sharing" in the app.
  • The driver can clear or change their name and phone number in the app's Settings at any time.
  • Location permission can be revoked from the device's system settings at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
